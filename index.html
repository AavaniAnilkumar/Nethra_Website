About Nethra Eyecare & Opticals

In the dynamic city of Pattambi, we at Nethra Eyecare Opticals take great delight in revolutionizing customer happiness and service in the optical sector. With more than 25 years of experience, we are dedicated to providing cutting-edge eye care solutions that improve each client's experience.

Nethra Eyecare Opticals is a respected member of the well-known Popular Opticals family, which has a long history that includes prestigious brands like as Nethra Eye Hospital, Brothers Opticals, Popular Opticals, and Eye Point Opticals. Our path has been one of constant expansion, inventiveness, and quality focused on the needs of the consumer.

We are pleased to provide the widest selection of both domestic and foreign eyewear brands, with an emphasis on delivering unmatched eyewear and optical services.



Categories

Eye Glasses

Eyeglasses are a pair of lenses mounted in a frame you wear on your face so you can see better.

Screen Glass

Computer glasses reduce glare, enhance contrast, and block blue light. They improve posture by allowing comfortable screen distance.

Sunglass

Sunglasses are an important type of eyewear that help to protect the eyes from UV rays emitted by the sun, as well as potentially harmful elements such as wind.

Contact Lense

Contact lenses are thin, round lenses that rest on the surface of your eyes to help you see more clearly.
